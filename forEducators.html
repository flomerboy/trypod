lesson plans

Below, find a list of innovative ways that teachers can use the trypod system to make a difference in their class room.

  • after-class reflection: (10 minutes, all grade levels) After a class period, use AirPlay in "presentation" mode and ask students to make observations based off of what they see in the images. Sample questions: "What are the postures of learning here?" "Who looks like they are working together?" "How did the prototype change from start to finish?"
  • What were you thinking about? (10 minutes, K-4) Show the class 3-4 of the images from class time. Ask them to write down or draw what they were thinking about at the time of that picture.
  • How would you have done it differently? (10 minutes, 5-12) Review all of the images from class time and ask students to write down how they would have prototyped differently, now that they look back on it.
