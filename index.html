A system for documenting and sharing process.

What is trypod?

Trypod is a customizable physical device combined with an app running on your smartphone that work together to document process. Teachers can use this to help them keep track of project-based learning. Students can reflect on their process to become more efficient and work smarter.

the device

You can customize trypod by selecting different types of connectors and arms. Trypod is a DIY kit that you can buy, or source the materials yourself!


get started >

the app

step 1 : setup

set up the app by selecting how frequently to snap a photo, and how long you want it to run for.

step 2 : in progress

trypod will take photos at whatever interval you specify until the predetermined time ends.

step 3 : selecting and reflecting

After collecting all your precious photos, select the best ones to publish.

step 4 : share!

After you've selected the photos that best represent your session, give them a short caption and share with the world! #trypod

who is trypod for?

Whether you're an educator who wants to do more project-based lessons, a student, or a professional, trypod is for you! Get started today.
